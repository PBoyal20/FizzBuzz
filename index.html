Welcome to FizzBuzz

What is FizzBuzz?

FizzBuzz is a simple programming exercise often used in interviews. It involves printing a sequence of numbers from 1 to a given number, but with a twist: for multiples of 3, print "Fizz," for multiples of 5, print "Buzz," and for multiples of both 3 and 5, print "FizzBuzz."

Technologies Used

Example:

If the given number is 15, the FizzBuzz sequence will look like this: 

                1
                2
                Fizz
                4
                Buzz
                Fizz
                7
                8
                Fizz
                Buzz
                11
                Fizz
                13
                14
                FizzBuzz
